Online Healthcare Diagnostic Platform

Online Healthcare Diagnostic Platform [OHDP] is an Online virtual diagnostic platform where the user can check his/her ailments for Multiple diseases. OHDP can be used as a Medical Second Opinion Platform for Disease Diagnosis and Prognosis. OHDP idea will certainly be a game changer in Healthcare industry yet there are lot of limitations in its deployment for commercial use.
Our OHDP currently is operational with 3 diseases diagnostic care, namely - Skin Cancer, Tuberculosis, and Heart Diseases.

OHDP AI Products


    Skin Lesion Classifier : AI algorithm to identify 7 different skin lesions from Dermatoscopy Images
    Tuberculosis Diagnoser : AI algorithm to diagnose Tuberculosis Chest-Xray from normal ones
    Heart Risk Analyser : AI Prognostic algorithm to identify risk of heart diseases


AI in Healthcare

Artificial Intelligence in Healthcare has many amazing applications, yet is still limited and as mainly it is not preferred for diagnosis due to lack of quality data and Doctors approval. However, still in the modern digital era people are not able to get cheap and effective treatments. Advent of AI into Healthcare will sort such issues and will reduce the disease diagnosis time drastically.
